# flow-review — product lens

Read-only power-user critique. Produce findings; do not edit.

## Discover
Read `SPECIFICATIONS.md` (what's built), `CLAUDE.md` (capabilities), any user-feedback sources.

## Review
Power-user perspective — what's frustrating? what's missing? what's over-complicated? where are likely drop-off points? what would make this 10/10 vs 7/10?

## Report
5–10 specific, prioritized observations, each with a concrete suggestion. Top items can be drafted as specs with `/flow --add`.
