# flow:review — marketing lens

Read-only PMM audit of positioning and copy. Produce findings; do not edit.

## Discover
Find marketing content: landing page, `MARKETING.md`, any positioning docs.

## Review
PMM lens — does the positioning communicate clear value? Is the target audience explicit? Are features described as user outcomes, not implementation? Is pricing (if any) clear and justified?

## Report
Positioning gaps + specific copy improvements, prioritized. For each, a concrete suggested rewrite.
